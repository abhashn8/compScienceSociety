The Computer Science Society is a society primarily for computer science students and for those who have a strong interest in the field.

CLUB'S CONSTITUTION

OBJECTIVES

This club provides members the opportunity to learn more about the computer science field and many types of hands-on experience. Being given this opportunity may benefit members in their upcoming futures. This club may also prepare computer science students for their upcoming computer science courses.

During Club Meeting, members:

  • Learn about many applications and how to code.

    • - That is learning many programming languages such as c++, java, c# (c sharp), and html in order to create computer programs. Members also learn about many applications such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and Powerpoint.

  • Gain experience through networking.

    • - That is learning from other members experiences in the club. (Members may go up in front of the room to lecture for at least 15 minutes during club meetings.) For example: If one member has experience with java, he or she may teach the rest of the club a little bit about java.

  • Learn from professionals.

    • - That is learning from the experiences of those who work for many companies such as Cisco, IBM, and Microsoft, through club events.

  • Learn more about the field of computer science.

    • - Members learn about the many types of benefits that a computer science degree may offer and the types of the careers that are held within the field.
